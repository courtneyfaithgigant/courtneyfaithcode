WHO

Hello, Sweet Lovebeam.

I could tell you I have all the secrets to your best life: twelve Mercedes, a lifetime supply of Lululemon, a beach house crib on the Italian coast, and a Channing Tatum. However, you'd know that would be the largest piece of bullshit you'd ever seen.

This is not a space where someone can "fix" your life. I cannot mend your wounds or fill your aching, internal void. Nothing and no one can do that for you. Why? Only your own personal brand of compassion, forgiveness, and love can do that. But believe me, I know it's easier to point the finger. Someone else broke me so, someone else can fix me. The only problem is, one day, everyone will be gone... and the only thing you have left to point at is yourself.

And that's how I arrived here. I could no longer remain the helpless victim in my life's story. I had to open up to the darkness of my past. I had to surrender. Facing abuse, eating disorders, abandonment, anxiety, depression, PTSD, and one too many toxic relationships is never an easy or simple tale to tell... but, I say what happens next. It is my birthright to reclaim my power and define my path. That's why, in each moment of every day, I have to actively decide two things: (1) No matter what, I will love myself the best I can, and (2) Nothing and no one can stop me.


You should know, this work is hard as fuck. However, I am here with you as we untangle the lies and redefine the truth, together. All discovered wisdom jewels will be posted here for you to utilize or discard as you please. My hope is simply to inspire and bring confidence that you too can begin this journey towards radical, blissful self-love. It's time to break-free from the bullshit and embrace who we really are, revolutionary lovebeams.


And remember, you were born for this shit.
