ABOUT

Hello hello! I'm Courtney Faith.

What I Will Not Be Serving
I could tell you I have all the secrets to your best life, including but not limited to: two Mercedes, a beach house crib, and a Channing Tatum. However, you'd know that would be the largest, mushiest piece of BS you'd ever seen.

I could try to sell you on a never-before-seen, Full-Body Tone Blast and an EXCLUSIVE FREE GIFT (which, by the way, is just a coupon to another exercise you'll probably hate). And again, you'd know that was a whopping pile of smelly BS.

Who Will Be Hosting
I am here to share with you the insight and wisdom I collect exploring my own path. Stepping out of abuse, two eating disorders, abandonment, anxiety, depression, PTSD, and one too many toxic relationships is never an easy or simple tale to tell. However, I am here, fucking owning my shit.

Frankly, in each moment of every day, I have to actively decide two things: (1) I fucking rock, and (2) My life is the manifestation of "kicking ass."

What Is On The Menu
As I unravel my own life, any discovered insight will be posted here for you to utilize or discard as you please. My hope is simply to inspire, and bring confidence that you too can begin this journey towards radical, blissful self-love.

Disclaimer
This is not a space where someone can "fix" your life. I cannot mend your wounds or fill your aching, internal void. Nothing and no one can do that for you. Why? Only your own personal brand of compassion, forgiveness, and love can do that.

The path we are all on is rocky, weird, and messy as fuck. However, I am here with you as we walk it together. And let me just say, what a marvelous step we are taking today.
